What is Bathroom Stall?

Bathroom Stall lets you write about people you know.
So let the world know how awesome your people are... or aren't.
Use the top-right nav bar to get started reading or writing.

What isn't Bathroom Stall?

BS isn't a place to post discriminatory or baseless comments
about people. Use a real bathroom stall for that!
Feel free to contact us with concerns.
